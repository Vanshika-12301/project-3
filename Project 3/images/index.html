PARTNERING IN YOUR PROGRESS

Incorporated in 2002, VCS is India's leading provider of Engineering and Project Management Consultancy services (EPMC), construction supervision, compliance assessment, Testing, and Third-Party Inspection in cross-country pipelines and City Gas distribution networks, VCS also undertakes specialist investigative studies and provides technical advisory related to integrity and asset life-cycle management.VCS provides these services across Hydrocarbon (Onshore and Offshore), Power, Infrastructure, Marine, alternate energy, and general engineering indistries

Our highly competent workforce of over 450 professionals from various Engineering disciplines delivers services to our customers in India and internationally.

VCS is a certified ISO 9001:2015 company, and a NABCB accredited Inspection Body under ISO/IEC 17020:2012. We are also a member of the Federation of Indian Petroleum Industry (FIPI) and have the requisite pre-qualification & empanelment with other major government and private organizations.

Training services are provided through our subsidiary Energlobe Academy.
